# Cohort Conversion Rates
Results:
- Best performing cohort: **2021-01-18** → **11.17%**
- Worst performing cohort: **2021-02-01** → **4.14%**
- Most cohorts convert between **6% – 7.5%**

Interpretation:
Conversion performance varies significantly by cohort, showing that traffic quality and user intent change week to week.

Product Actions:
- Analyze what drove the 2021-01-18 cohort’s success and replicate it  
- Investigate acquisition channels and UX changes for low-performing cohorts  
- Use cohort tracking to measure future product and marketing improvements

# Time to Value (First-Session Behavior)
Results:
- High Intent users: 731 users, avg time to purchase **27.88 hours**
- Browsing users: 251 users, avg time to purchase **77.43 hours**
- Low Activity users: 87 users, avg time to purchase **93.62 hours**

Interpretation:
Users who view multiple products and add to cart in their first session convert much faster. First-session engagement is a strong predictor of quick purchase.

Product Actions:
- Identify High Intent users early
- Trigger fast checkout nudges and offers for them
- Use reminders and remarketing for Browsing and Low Activity users

# Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Results:
- Total customers: **1,069**
- Average orders per customer: **1.04**
- Average CLV: **$53.60**
- Max CLV: **$1,200**

Interpretation:
Most customers buy only once, but repeat buyers are worth about **2x more** than one-time buyers. A very small group of users drives disproportionately high value.

Product Actions:
- Focus on converting one-time buyers into repeat buyers
- Introduce loyalty or rewards programs
- Retarget past buyers with personalized offers
- Protect and nurture high-value customers
