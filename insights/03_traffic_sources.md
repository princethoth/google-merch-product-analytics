- (data deleted): **1.16 sessions**

Interpretation:

- Use retargeting more aggressively for Google traffic
- Optimize search landing pages to shorten the decision cycle

# New vs Returning Users
Results:
- Returning users:
  - Viewers: 10,008  
  - Purchasers: 1,069  
  - Conversion rate: **10.68%**

- New users:
  - Viewers: 7,209  
  - Purchasers: 0  
  - Conversion rate: **0%**

Interpretation:
All purchases come from returning users. New users do not convert at all in this dataset, which means buying is heavily dependent on prior exposure and familiarity with the store.

Product Meaning:
This product relies strongly on repeat visits before users feel confident enough to purchase. Trust, brand recognition, and repeated engagement are critical to conversion.

Product Actions:
- Focus on remarketing and retargeting campaigns  
- Capture user emails early for follow-up  
- Improve trust signals for first-time visitors  
- Add onboarding elements that reduce friction for new users  

# Conversion by Device
Results:
- Tablet: **9.15%** (highest)
- Mobile: **8.35%**
- Desktop: **7.58%** (lowest)

Interpretation:
All devices convert well, but tablets and mobile outperform desktop. This suggests users on mobile and tablet are more purchase-intent driven, while desktop users may be more exploratory.

Product Actions:
- Prioritize mobile and tablet UX optimization
- Improve desktop checkout flow to reduce friction
- Run device-specific A/B tests, especially on desktop
