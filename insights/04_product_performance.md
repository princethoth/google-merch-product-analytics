# Top Converting Products
Results:
- Best converting product: **Google Keychain (28.77%)**
- Other high performers:
  - Google Emoji Magnet Set (25.00%)
  - Google Frisbee (24.07%)
  - Google PNW Campus Mug (16.92%)
  - Google Lapel Pin (15.09%)

Interpretation:
Small, low-cost, and novelty items convert the best. These products are impulse-friendly and require less purchase consideration.

Product Actions:
- Promote these products on the homepage
- Use them in ads and entry-level bundles
- Position them as add-on items to increase basket size

# Cart Abandonment by Product
Results:
Several products show a **100% cart abandonment rate**, including:
- #IamRemarkable Ladies T-Shirt (268 users)
- Candy Cane Android Cardboard Sculpture (281 users)
- Android Iconic Mug Gray (244 users)
- Android Iconic Notebook (108 users)

Interpretation:
These products generate strong interest (users add them to cart) but fail to convert entirely. This suggests major friction such as pricing concerns, shipping costs, low perceived value, or unclear product information.

Product Actions:
- Review pricing and shipping for these items
- Improve product images and descriptions
- Add reviews or social proof
- Test discounts or bundling to increase conversions

# Product Affinity
Results:
Top product pairs frequently bought together:
- Google Black Cloud Zip Hoodie + Google Metallic Notebook Set (29)
- Google Metallic Notebook Set + Google Thermal Tumbler Navy (27)
- Google Black Cloud Zip Hoodie + Google Thermal Tumbler Navy (25)
- Google Crewneck Sweatshirt Green + Google Sherpa Zip Hoodie Navy (10)

Interpretation:
Apparel items are often purchased together with accessories like notebooks and tumblers, showing strong cross-category bundling behavior.

Product Actions:
- Create bundles combining apparel + accessories
- Add “Frequently Bought Together” suggestions on product pages
- Use these pairs in upsell prompts during checkout
